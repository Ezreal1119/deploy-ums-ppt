PAIN POINTS · Four Major Challenges

Core Challenges
in Enterprise Device Management

01 · Device Control
Scattered devices
decentralized management
Devices are spread across sites, making status invisible and configurations inconsistent.
→ Remote centralized management · Batch group operations
02 · App Governance
Chaotic app distribution
uncontrolled version management
Self-installed apps and version drift make business software hard to control.
→ Enterprise app store · Silent installation · Blacklist & whitelist
03 · Data Security
Data breaches
& device loss risks
Lost devices, unsafe networks, USB copying, and unauthorized apps increase leakage risk.
→ Remote wipe · Geofencing · Permission lockdown
04 · O&M Efficiency
High O&M costs
slow incident response
On-site troubleshooting is slow and costly; issues are often found too late.
→ Remote desktop · Real-time monitoring · One-click diagnostics
